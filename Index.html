ABOUT US

Learn More About Us

Moontan was founded with a passion for bringing people together over delicious, healthy food. Our chefs craft each dish with care, using only the freshest ingredients to ensure a memorable dining experience.

  • Authentic recipes passed down through generations.
  • Locally sourced ingredients for ultimate freshness.
  • A welcoming atmosphere for friends and family.

We strive to create a place where everyone can enjoy great food, friendly service, and a sense of community. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a special occasion, Moontan is your home for flavor and hospitality.
